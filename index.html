Help & FAQs

Find answers to common questions about the IIBS IT Service Ticketing System.

Click on "Raise Ticket" in the navigation bar or the "Raise a Ticket" button on the home page. Fill in your personal details (Name, IIBS ID, Role, Department, Contact, Email), select the service request type, and click Submit.

You can raise tickets for: Laptop With/Without Charger, Desktop Not Working, Panel Board, WiFi Not Connecting, Network Issues, Printer Not Working, Colour Ink Refill, Tonner Replacement, System Not Working/Powering ON/Slow, HDMI Cable Request, Keyboard & Mouse Replacement, Wireless Mouse, and Other Requests.

Go to the "Track Tickets" tab. You can search by ticket ID, name, or keyword. Use the filters to narrow results by status, service type, or role.

If your issue doesn't match any of the predefined categories, select "Request for Other". A text box will appear where you can describe your specific requirement in detail.

Resolution times depend on the type and complexity of the request. Simple requests like laptop/charger are typically handled within 2-4 hours. Hardware repairs may take 24-48 hours. You can track real-time status updates in the Track Tickets section.

For urgent or critical issues, please contact the IIBS IT Helpdesk directly at the numbers listed below, or raise a ticket through this portal for tracking purposes.

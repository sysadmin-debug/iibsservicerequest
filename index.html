Help & FAQs

Find answers to common questions about raising and tracking service requests.

Click on "Raise Request" in the navigation bar or the "Raise a Request" button on the home page. Fill in your details, select the service category, describe the issue, and click Submit. You'll receive a ticket ID for tracking.

Go to the "Track Requests" tab. You can search by your ticket ID, name, or keyword. Use filters to narrow down by status, category, or priority.

Low: Minor inconvenience, not urgent.
Medium: Affecting your work but you can continue.
High: Work is blocked, needs urgent attention.
Critical: Emergency or safety issue requiring immediate response.

IT Support: Hardware, Software, Network/Internet, Email/Account Access
Facilities: Electrical, Plumbing, AC/HVAC, Furniture/Infrastructure
Other: Security/CCTV, Housekeeping, Transport, and more

Response times vary by priority:
Critical: Within 1 hour
High: Within 4 hours
Medium: Within 24 hours
Low: Within 48 hours

For critical emergencies, please contact the IIBS Admin Office directly or raise a request with "Critical" priority. The support team monitors critical tickets on priority.
